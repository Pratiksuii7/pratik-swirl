Hidden Gems of Nepal

Nepal is the most beautiful country rich in its history, diversity and beauty. Nepal is naturally blessed. Every year thousands of tourists come to Nepal to visit the natural beauty of Himalayas, rivers, lakes and green valleys.

Nepal hides much history, diversity and beauty. Some of the hidden gems of Nepal are elaborated below:

1. Panch Pokhari

Panch Pokhari is a permit-free trekking destination. Panch Pokhari is a group of 5 holy lakes in the Sindhupalchok district of Nepal. It is the 9th highest altitude wetland in the world. The lake is a popular destination for Hindu and Buddhist pilgrims during Janaipurnima.

2. Guerilla Trail

The Guerilla trek trail is one of the newly-discovered hidden gems in Nepal. This trekking route follows the path used by Maoist rebels during the armed conflict. The trail starts from Beni near Pokhara and ends in Jelbang in Rolpa.

4. Upper Dolpo Trek

Upper Dolpo Trek is one of the most remote and challenging treks in Nepal, hence one of the greatest hidden gems in Nepal, that takes you to the remotest parts of western Nepal. Apart from the glorious views of mountains, hills and lakes, the trek also gives people a great opportunity to experience the local culture and lifestyle of people living there.

5. Badimalika

The Badimalika region, as one of the hidden gems in Nepal, is also untouched by modernisation and offers a unique opportunity for trekkers to experience the remote and unspoiled beauty of the Himalayas.
